Overview

Joo Studio is a creative brand founded and managed by Yunsun Joo, specializing in character-based merchandise and visual storytelling. With a focus on whimsical aesthetics, the studio turns original characters into physical products such as enamel pins, stickers, patterned textiles, apparel graphics, and art prints. Rooted in a passion for charm and emotional connection, Joo Studio’s designs have attracted fans and collectors seeking joyful, expressive visuals for everyday life.
