JooStudio

Joo Studio is a creative brand founded by Yunsun Joo, specializing in adorable character designs that are transformed into merchandise for sale. The studio focuses on crafting enamel pins, stickers, pattern designs, T-shirt graphics, and decorative posters for interior spaces. With a passion for charming and playful aesthetics, Joo Studio continues to expand its product lineup. Future projects include lanyards, more diverse enamel pin designs, and zines, bringing even more delightful creations to fans and collectors alike.
Stay tuned for new releases and exciting designs from Joo Studio!
