stylewise:Pick

AI-powered outfit coordination platform using color theory and personalized style matching.

Overview

StyleWise : Pick is a capstone project that reimagines outfit coordination through AI. The app helps users make smarter fashion decisions by scanning their existing wardrobe or shopping finds and offering intelligent pairing suggestions — powered by color theory and personal style preferences.

Team

Myself (UI/UX Designer)

User Research / Persona Development / Visual Design / Prototyping

  • Designed visual identity and UI components

Research Insights

"I usually go for what feels comfortable… but it’d be nice to look a little more put-together.”
  • AI Color Matching - Recommends outfits based on color theory and wardrobe data
  • On-the-Go Suggestions - Scan clothing to instantly receive coordination ideas
  • Integrated Shopping - Links to online stores for seamless purchase of missing items
  • Style Confidence - Encourages experimentation and self-expression

Research Summary

  • User Behavior Insight - Online shoppers primarily prioritize size and comfort, often resulting in returns due to poor fit.
  • Identified Gap - Surprisingly, most users overlook color as a key decision factor.
  • Proposed Solution - StyleWise introduces AI-powered color coordination to improve outfit selection and elevate styling confidence.
  • Future Vision - 3D modeling and body shape personalization will allow virtual try-ons, empowering users to rediscover joy and self-expression post body changes (e.g. postpartum).
