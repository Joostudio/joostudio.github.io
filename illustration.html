Overview

I enjoy capturing emotions and little stories through lines, color, and rhythm. From character-driven portraits to editorial pieces, storybook pages, and playful cartoon moments - each illustration is a snapshot of feeling, movement, or a tiny world of its own. Whether thoughtful, dreamy, or downright silly, my work celebrates expression in all its forms.

I focus on capturing the personality and mood of the characters by using a limited color palette. The goal is to emphasize the essence of each character while maintaining a cohesive and bold visual style.
