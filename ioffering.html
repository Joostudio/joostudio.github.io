iOffering

iOffering is a digital platform for church donations , allowing members to contribute online instead of traditional cash or check donations. This system simplifies the donation process for both donors and church administrators, ensuring a more convenient, secure, and automated way to give.


Role
Visual design
Prototyping & Testing

Key Features & Benefits
Convenience → Church members can donate anytime, anywhere, without needing to carry cash
Automated Processing → Reduces manual tracking efforts for church staff
Recurring Donations → Supports automatic monthly contributions for consistent giving
Secure Transactions → Ensures safe and transparent donation processing


Project : Recurring Donation Auto-Payment


Problem: Previously, users could only make one-time donations, which was inconvenient for those wanting to donate regularly
Design Solution: Introduced a recurring donation feature, allowing users to set up automated monthly payments without manual intervention
