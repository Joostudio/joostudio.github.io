iOffering

Designing a smoother, recurring donation experience for churches and their communities.

Overview

iOffering is a digital donation platform that allows church members to give online, replacing traditional cash or check methods. The system simplifies contributions for both donors and church administrators, enabling a more convenient, secure, and automated giving process. As a product-focused designer, I worked on improving the donation flow and prototyping features that reduce repetitive tasks for staff while increasing consistency in member giving.

My Role

Visual Design / Prototyping & Testing
I was responsible for the visual design of the recurring donation feature and collaborated closely with the development team to refine usability and ensure smooth integration into the existing system.

What I Worked On

  • Identified the need for recurring donations to support consistent giving
  • Designed a donation flow that allows members to set up monthly contributions from anywhere, without carrying cash
  • Created a clear, minimal-step interface to help users feel in control while supporting automation
  • Focused on reducing manual tracking for church staff through automated transaction records
  • Ensured all donation flows were secure, transparent, and mobile-friendly

Recurring Donation Auto-Payment

Problem: Previously, users could only make one-time donations. Members who wanted to give regularly had to return each time and manually complete the process—resulting in drop-offs and inconsistent giving behavior.

Solution: I designed and prototyped a recurring donation feature that allows users to set up monthly contributions with just a few steps. The interface offers a clear confirmation flow, letting users review and manage their automated giving at any time. This change not only improves the donor experience but also reduces manual tracking and communication overhead for church staff.

Results

  • Made regular giving easier and more consistent for members
  • Reduced administrative work by eliminating repetitive donation tracking
  • Increased user satisfaction by simplifying the donation process
  • Improved the platform’s competitiveness with modern fintech-style experiences
