StyleSnap

StyleSnap(Solo Project) is a automated product content tool designed to streamline retail seller workflows by providing instant product image analysis and recommendation. It automates background removal, color correction, and image optimization to reduce repetitive work and accelerate product photo readiness.

Role
Secondary Research
Survey & User Interviews
Persona Creation
Wireframing & Prototyping
Prototyping & Testing
Visual Design

Key Features & Benefits
Automated Editing → AI removes backgrounds and enhances images automatically.
Time-saving Workflow → Speeds up product photography for busy retail sellers
Scalable Output → Ideal for large inventories needing uniform, high-quality visuals
Platform-ready Images → Generates content optimized for Amazon, Etsy, Shopify, etc
Improved Product Display → Delivers professional and consistent images across channels
