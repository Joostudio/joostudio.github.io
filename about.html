about me

Hi, I’m Yunsun Joo

Hi, I’m Yunsun Joo — a Product Designer & Illustrator with 6+ years of experience in digital product design, motion graphics, and visual storytelling.

I design to bring clarity where there’s chaos, and beauty where there’s complexity. From reducing support volume by 90% to streamlining onboarding and checkout flows, I build intuitive systems that feel effortless — because real users deserve solutions that simply work.

As an illustrator, I bring a deep sense of story, rhythm, and emotion into every interface. I don’t just design screens — I craft experiences that people can trust, navigate with ease, and connect with.

I live at the intersection of structure and feeling, logic and empathy. To me, design isn’t just how it looks — it’s how it thinks, and how it makes people feel. And I’m focused on practical, scalable solutions — for both users and teams.

I care about building products that solve real problems with precision, and empower the people who use them — with grace.

🎯 What I Believe

I don’t just design screens - I fix broken systems.
When things don't work, I don’t wait for permission - I ask the hard questions, build the missing pieces, and make it better.
Because good design doesn’t just look good - it works, lasts , and empowers.

⚙️ How I Work

I don’t wait for instructions - I identify problems and move fast to fix them. I’ve worked as a bridge between users, sales, and product - and I naturally stepped into product owner roles when none existed.
I value clarity, process, and speed. I design systems that reduce errors, eliminate friction, and help teams work smarter - not harder.

🤝 Collaboration Style

I work well in cross-functional teams, especially in fast-paced or ambiguous environments. I’m not afraid to speak up, but I always aim for respectful, solution-oriented communication. I thrive when I can partner with engineers, marketers, and customer-facing teams to make real impact.
