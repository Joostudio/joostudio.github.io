StyleWise

StyleWise(Capstone Project) is a AI-powered outfit coordination platform that suggests outfit combinations based on color theory and user style preferences. It helps users elevate their wardrobe by intelligently matching items they already own or are considering purchasing.

Research Summery

To improve the overall user experience of iOrderFoods, I conducted competitor analysis, user interviews, and prototype testing to optimize the platform.

User Behavior Insight: User research revealed that online shoppers primarily prioritize size and comfort, which often results in returns due to poor fit.
Identified Gap: Interestingly, all survey participants overlooked the role of color in their shopping decisions, focusing almost entirely on functional aspects like sizing.
Proposed Solution: StyleWise introduces the often-neglected element of color into the outfit selection process by providing AI-powered styling suggestions based on color theory. The platform helps users instantly elevate their style through smart color coordination—without compromising on comfort.
Future Vision: StyleWise plans to incorporate 3D body modeling based on user-inputted data (e.g., weight, body shape) to enable real-time virtual try-ons, significantly reducing return rates. In particular, for users whose body shape has changed—such as after childbirth—StyleWise aims to provide renewed confidence and joy through color-based styling that honors both comfort and individuality.
