StyleWise

StyleWise (Capstone Project) is an AI-powered outfit coordination platform that suggests outfit combinations based on color theory and user style preferences. It helps users elevate their wardrobe by intelligently matching items they already own or are considering purchasing.

Key Features & Benefits
AI Color Matching → Suggests outfit combinations based on the user’s wardrobe and color theory
On-the-go Convenience → Users can scan clothing items and receive instant recommendations
Integrated Shopping → Links to online stores, enabling users to purchase recommended items
Convenience → Users can easily scan and receive outfit suggestions on the go

Research Summary

To improve the overall user experience of iOrderFoods, I conducted competitor analysis, user interviews, and prototype testing to optimize the platform.

User Behavior Insight: Online shoppers primarily prioritize size and comfort, often resulting in returns due to poor fit.

Identified Gap: Surprisingly, most users overlook color as a key decision factor.

Proposed Solution: StyleWise introduces AI-powered color coordination to improve outfit selection and elevate styling confidence.

Future Vision: 3D modeling and body shape personalization will allow virtual try-ons, empowering users to rediscover joy and self-expression post body changes (e.g. postpartum).
