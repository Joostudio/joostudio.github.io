StyleWise

StyleWise is a AI-powered outfit coordination platform that suggests outfit combinations based on color theory and user style preferences.

Role

Prototyping & Testing

Key Features & Benefits
Convenience → Church members can donate anytime, anywhere, without needing to carry cash
AI Color Matching → Suggests outfit combinations based on the user’s wardrobe and color theory
Convenience → Users can easily scan and receive outfit suggestions on the go
Integrated Shopping → Links to online stores, enabling users to purchase recommended items
