stylewise:Pick

AI-powered outfit coordination platform using color theory and personalized style matching.

Overview

StyleWise is a capstone project that reimagines outfit coordination through AI. The app helps users make smarter fashion decisions by scanning their existing wardrobe or shopping finds and offering intelligent pairing suggestions — powered by color theory and personal style preferences.
Whether someone is recovering from a body transformation (e.g. postpartum) or simply looking to express themselves more confidently, StyleWise provides personalized, frictionless guidance for everyday styling.

Research Insights

To validate the product vision, I conducted:

  • Secondary research
  • Surveys with 20 users across gender and age groups
  • User interviews with 5 participants

Insight 1: Most shoppers prioritize size and comfort but overlook color coordination.

Insight 2: Many users feel overwhelmed when building outfits, especially after body changes.

Research Summary

To improve the overall user experience of iOrderFoods, I conducted competitor analysis, user interviews, and prototype testing to optimize the platform.

User Behavior Insight: Online shoppers primarily prioritize size and comfort, often resulting in returns due to poor fit.

Identified Gap: Surprisingly, most users overlook color as a key decision factor.

Proposed Solution: StyleWise introduces AI-powered color coordination to improve outfit selection and elevate styling confidence.

Future Vision: 3D modeling and body shape personalization will allow virtual try-ons, empowering users to rediscover joy and self-expression post body changes (e.g. postpartum).
